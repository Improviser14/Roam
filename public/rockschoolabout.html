If you join our Rock School program, built into the tuition is the opportunity to perform in real-time in front of a real audience, often at one of the best venues in the area. Our bands regularly perform at The Birchmere and The State Theatre as examples.

Please visit our site's internal social app roamHangout to join our community, even if you don't study with us. Feel free to create a free account, create a thread, post a comment, interact with others, and see for yourself why we really are different than other schools.
