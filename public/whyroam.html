What makes ROAM different?

Rock of Ages Music, founded by Alexandria native John Patrick, opened it's doors in the month of September, 2011. ROAM is conveniently located in the heart of Alexandria's vibrant Del Ray neighborhood, just outside of Washington, D.C..

At ROAM with begin with the understanding that each individual is different, and that each student has their own unique abilities, tastes, and learning styles. Because of this, our curriculum stays agile. Our students receive custom lessons designed to fit their needs, and our instructors often adjust the lessons to account for changing needs. Unlike other schools that follow a script, we know that music and students of music don't learn in a static environment. Although math and structure are fundamental to music (and most everything for that matter!); in practice, music is an art. Art, at it's highest level, comes with the ability to improvise and adjust to one's environment in real-time. Because of this, each band and each musician (including instructors) should work on their ability to adjust. This is how one finds one's musical voice, and how unique styles are forged. Plus, it's fun! We teach from this perspective.

All of our instructors teach the fundamentals of theory, ear training, and technique and strive to cultivate each student's style and musical voice. Each teacher, because they are highly-skilled professional musicians, are given autonomy within the ROAM structure to create lesson plans that cater to the individual student.

We are a family, and we strive to maintain a sense of community here at ROAM and within the Del Ray Community as a whole. We believe in creating a sense of belonging through mentorship, teamwork, friendship, kindness, fun, and we always try to adhere to the concept of growth-mindset. From a higher-level overview, we are all working to improve together, because we are all students of this beautiful art, music.

If you join our Rock School program, built into the tuition is the opportunity to perform in real-time in front of a real audience, often at one of the best venues in the area. Our bands regularly perform at The Birchmere and The State Theatre as examples.

Please visit our site's internal social app roamHangout to join our community, even if you don't study with us. Feel free to create a free account, create a thread, post a comment, interact with others, and see for yourself why we really are different than other schools.
